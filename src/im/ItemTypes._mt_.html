Convexys is a solution to manage Parts, their Bills of Materials, as well as their documents. Here is an overview of the features of the application.

Physical Item

Any physical part, assembly, kit, or bundle that is not described by the types above.

Digital Item

Any digital asset such as document, software, picture, video ...

Service

A service or labor

Reference

A reference to information that is not a sellable asset.
